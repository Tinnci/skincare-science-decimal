# Skincare Knowledge Base - Johnny.Decimal System

This project uses the Johnny.Decimal system to organize skincare knowledge, research, and product data.

## Areas & Categories

### [10-19] Ingredients & Science
Focuses on the biochemical properties, mechanisms, and classifications of skincare actives.
- **11 Retinoids**
  - 11.01 Introduction to Retinoids & Generations
  - 11.02 Mechanism of Action: Retinoic Acid Receptors (RARs)
  - 11.03 Clinical Efficacy: Comparative Study Results
  - 11.04 Industry Context: Why Cosmetics Aren't "Drugs"
  - 11.05 Evaluation Framework: The Lab Muffin Matrix

### [20-29] Skin Concerns
Focuses on specific skin conditions and the theoretical approaches to treating them.
- **21 Hyperpigmentation**
  - 21.01 Pathophysiology & Treatment Strategies

### [30-39] Products & Reviews
Database of specific commercial products, categorized by their primary active ingredients.
- **31 Retinoid Products**
  - 31.01 Product Recommendations for Hyperpigmentation

---

## Naming Convention
- **Folders**: `[Category ID]_[Category Name]` (e.g., `11_Retinoids`)
- **Files**: `[ID]_[Description].[extension]` (e.g., `11.01_Generations.typ`)
