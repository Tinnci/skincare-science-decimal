## ðŸ“‚ Structure (Johnny.Decimal)
This project follows the Johnny.Decimal system (10-19 for science, 20-29 for concerns, 30-39 for products).
- **10-19 Ingredients & Science**: Active ingredients, receptor mechanisms, and chemical generations.
- **20-29 Skin Concerns**: Focus on specific conditions like Hyperpigmentation or Acne.
- **30-39 Products & Reviews**: Database of product evaluations based on clinical reasoning.
